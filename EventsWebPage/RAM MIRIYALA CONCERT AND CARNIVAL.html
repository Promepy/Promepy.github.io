About the Event

What’s Happening at the Carnival?

The RAM Miriyala Concert & Carnival isn’t just about the music—it’s a day filled with festivities that everyone can enjoy. From thrilling games and delightful street food to mesmerizing cultural performances, the carnival is designed to entertain attendees of all ages. As you stroll through the vibrant avenues of the venue, you’ll feel the energy of live music and joyous celebration.

Meet Ram Miriyala

The Soulful Voice Behind Iconic Hits!

Known for his versatile vocal abilities and unique musical style, Ram Miriyala has captured hearts across genres. From soulful ghazals to energetic Bollywood hits, his performances bridge the gap between tradition and modernity. A rising star, Ram’s ability to connect with his audience through his heartfelt renditions has made him a household name in the music world.

Starting from humble beginnings, Ram's journey is an inspiring tale of passion and dedication. Over the years, he has amassed a loyal fanbase and gained recognition on prominent platforms. His live performances are known for their energy and emotional depth, making every concert an immersive experience. Don't miss the chance to witness this phenomenal artist live!
