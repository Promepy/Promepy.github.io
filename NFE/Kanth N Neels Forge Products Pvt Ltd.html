Neels Forge Products & Engg. Services

 Neels Forge Products Engineering Services was established in the year 2007. Neels Forge Products Engineering Services is one of the renowned companies engaged in offering ferrous & non ferrous forgings and precision machined components for automobiles & general engineering applications. M/s neel S forge products & engineering services located at autonagar, the industrial area of Visakhapatnam, for manufacturing of ferrous & non ferrous forgings & precision machined components. We have established full-fledged forging unit equipped with forging facility, tool room facility, facility for post forged operations & cnc machining.

All the manufacturing facility are run & managed by forge technologist, being expertise in our domain we offer quality forgings for the clients. Under the leadership of our mentors we have attained a special position in the market. We are known for the quality and that S why we are the foremost choice of our esteemed clients. Our varied range includes split flanges, 4 bolted flanges, 2 bolted flanges, elbows, tees and 4-way cross tee, for general engineering and hydraulic applications. We also offer customized forging requirements.
